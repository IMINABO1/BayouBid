The Problem

A community in crisis

COVID-19 + Hurricane Ida

Back-to-back disasters collapsed businesses across Lafourche Parish, devastating the local economy built around Port Fourchon.

Jobs Disappeared

Business closures wiped out W-2 employment — and with it, the employer-sponsored health insurance that families depended on.

IGS Score: 36

Census Tract 22057 020600 sits at a critical IGS of 36. Our data shows a +0.81 Pearson correlation between Economy Score and Health Insurance Coverage.

How It Works

AI-powered procurement, human-powered recovery

1
Match

AI scans SAM.gov and Louisiana LaPAC daily, matching RFPs to each business by capability, NAICS codes, certifications, and location.

2
Prepare

AI drafts capability statements and proposal sections. Advisors provide bid coaching, compliance support, and readiness checklists.

3
Fund

CDFI partners and SBA loan programs provide working capital and bridge funding so winners can actually deliver on contracts.

4
Consortium

For contracts over $500K, we assemble local business consortiums under teaming agreements to bid collectively on large opportunities.
