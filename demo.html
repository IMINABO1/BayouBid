How the system works
RFP Matching
An AI engine scans SAM.gov and Louisiana's LaPAC portal weekly, surfacing the top 5 contracts matched to each business by NAICS code, capability, and contract size.
Bid Readiness Guidance
Businesses see exactly which documents they have, which they are missing, and how to get each one — so no application stalls because of paperwork.
Fractional RFP Consortiums
For contracts above $500K, the system assembles 6–8 businesses into a formal consortium bid — distributing contract scope and revenue across the community.
